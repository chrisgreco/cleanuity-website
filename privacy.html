Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Cleanuity collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

When you use our booking form, we collect information necessary to provide our cleaning services, such as your name, address, contact details, and booking preferences. This information is processed through the BookingKoala platform.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to schedule and manage your cleaning appointments, communicate with you about your bookings, and process payments. We do not share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide our services (e.g., through BookingKoala).

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. Payment processing is handled securely by BookingKoala and Stripe.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of the website.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. Please contact us at hello@cleanuity.com if you wish to exercise these rights.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website.
