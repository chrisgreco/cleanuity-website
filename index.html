Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Cleanuity.

How do I book a cleaning?
Booking a cleaning with Cleanuity is simple. Click the "Book Now" button, select your preferred date and time, choose your cleaning package and any add-ons, select a cleaner (or let us match you with one), and confirm your booking. You'll receive an immediate confirmation email with all the details.
How does the Auto-Rebook feature work?
When booking a cleaning, you can opt into our Auto-Rebook feature and select your preferred frequency (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly). Before each scheduled cleaning, you'll receive a notification asking for one-click confirmation. This ensures you always have a cleaning scheduled without the hassle of rebooking each time.
Are your cleaners vetted and insured?
Yes, all Cleanuity professionals undergo a rigorous vetting process including background checks, reference verification, and in-person interviews. Additionally, all cleaners are fully insured, so you can have peace of mind knowing your home is in good hands.
How does the real-time tracking work?
On the day of your scheduled cleaning, you'll receive a notification when your cleaner is on their way. You can track their location in real-time through our app or website, similar to how you would track a food delivery. This feature helps you plan your day more effectively.
What is photo verification?
After completing the cleaning, your cleaner will take photos of key areas to verify the job has been completed to our high standards. These photos are sent to you immediately, allowing you to see the results even if you're not home. This is especially useful for remote homeowners or Airbnb hosts.
How do the smart home integrations work?
Cleanuity integrates with popular smart home systems like Amazon Key and SmartThings. During the booking process, you can set up secure, temporary access for your cleaner. The system automatically grants access only during your scheduled cleaning time and notifies you when they enter and exit your home.
What if I'm not satisfied with the cleaning?
Your satisfaction is our top priority. If you're not completely satisfied with your cleaning, contact us within 24 hours, and we'll send the cleaner back to address any issues at no additional cost. If you're still not satisfied, we'll provide a full refund.
Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?
No, our cleaners bring all necessary cleaning supplies and equipment. However, if you prefer specific products to be used in your home, you can provide them, and our cleaners will be happy to use them.
