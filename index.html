Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Cleanuity

How do I book a cleaning?

Booking is simple! Click the "Book Now" button, select your service type, add any extras you need, choose your preferred date and time, and provide entry instructions. The entire process takes less than 60 seconds.

How does the Auto-Rebook feature work?

After your first cleaning, you'll have the option to set up recurring cleanings with your preferred cleaner. You can choose weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly intervals. You'll receive a notification before each cleaning with the option to confirm with one click or reschedule if needed.

How does Smart Home integration work?

Cleanuity integrates with popular smart home systems like Amazon Key and SmartThings. During booking, you can select your smart home system and provide temporary access credentials. Our system automatically revokes access after the cleaning is complete for maximum security.

How does Photo Verification work?

After completing each cleaning, your cleaner will take photos of key areas to document the completed work. These photos are sent to you via email or the Cleanuity app, allowing you to verify the quality of the cleaning even when you're not home.

How does Real-Time Tracking work?

On the day of your cleaning, you'll receive real-time updates about your cleaner's ETA. Once they're en route, you can track their location and receive notifications when they arrive, similar to food delivery or rideshare apps.
